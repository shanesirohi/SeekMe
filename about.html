About Us

What is Seek ME

Seek ME is an app as well as web app which helps you connect with people who need job or people who need workers. You can list your worker requirement and leave rest to us. Your requirement can contain any occupation like Labour, Mechanic, Electrician. Mostly unproffesional work. You can also get a job for yourself for free near your locality for any unproffesional work you know.

How we bring change in lives

We help people by giving them job opportunities so they can have a better life. The work doesn't has to be proffesional work, it can be any work like electrician, mechanic, labour, helper etc. We help people who want to find somebody to work for them reducing their labour and waste of hours searching them.

Why use Seek ME

Seek Me not only supplies job opportunities but also teaches them basic skills required for a basic job. We give them free educational videos to watch to help them learn skills to help them. Also we find jobs in the locality of you so to reduce hassles.
